I saw the Waterloo Campus!

While visiting my sister from a nearby university, we decided to take a trip to see what the University of Waterloo is like...

Recent Events:
· · · (24/07/2022) Over the weekend, my family and I visited my sister who studies at the City of Waterloo. While chatting at her apartment, a brief idea sparked up. I would get a tour outside of University Waterloo (my dream school) to see what it's like. When I walked through the campus, I couldn't believe my eyes; all the buildings were giant and FUTURISTIC!!! Additionally, the campus was so big that a train ran through it so students could go from building-to-building faster (which were several minutes on foot). When I asked some of my sister's friends who have been through some UW engineering buildings, they said that it was even more tech-filled on the inside! Now, it's only a matter of time before I could attend the virtual tours coming in the future.

Local municipal elections are starting...

so that's why I created a website for a candidate!

Recent Projects:
· · · (01/09/2022) In my city, we have municipal elections coming up. This election determines who becomes city-councilor of my ward. Naturally, I found the opportunity to get volunteer hours; however, instead of publicising a candidate, I found it to be much more interesting if I would've created a website for one. On my city's official website, it has a list of candidates including their email, ward, name and website (if provided). After finding this out, I contacted several candidates who didn't have a website and asked if I could volunteer to give them a free website. While most of them declined, a woman named Carla Green emailed me back and we started work on the website. After I showed her wire-frames (the layout of the website) on Google Slides, I proceded to actually create it on Weebly. After several hours of work, I was finally able to complete it and the current version is linked below. This was so much fun and definitely worth it as I expanded my resume and got volunteer hours!

Link to the final product (as of September 1st, 2022): https://vote-carla-green.weebly.com/
